Verdict

The GT54 with the wick turned up: 3 kW peak, 338 Nm and ~49 mph from a mini frame. A pocket rocket, literally.

Motor & speed

Power comes from a 3,000W peak rear hub motor putting down 338 Nm of torque. Top speed is 40 mph (up to 49 mph claimed).

Battery & range

It carries a 60V 25Ah removable lithium (~1,500Wh, 21700 cells) battery. Manufacturer-rated range is ~60 miles — expect less if you ride it hard, uphill, or in the cold. A full charge takes ~8 hours (67.2V 3A fast charger).

Build, suspension & brakes

The bike weighs 102.5 lbs. Suspension: Double wishbone front with adjustable damping; ~1200 lb rear shock. Braking is handled by front and rear hydraulic discs + electronic brake. It rolls on 60/100-14 front, 60/100-12 rear motorcycle tires. Max rider load is 220 lbs.

Who it's for

Fit: Teens and adults (13+). Best for: fastest mini for the money. It is street legal.

Pros & Cons

  • Small wheels at 49 mph demand care
